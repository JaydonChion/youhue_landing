Find Your Colour Season

Everyone belongs to one of four seasons. Once you know yours, getting dressed becomes effortless — and way more fun.

🌸
Spring
Warm, bright & fresh. Think coral, peach, golden yellow, and soft greens.
🌊
Summer
Cool, soft & muted. Think lavender, dusty rose, powder blue, and mauve.
🍂
Autumn
Warm, rich & earthy. Think rust, burnt orange, olive, mustard, and brown.
❄️
Winter
Cool, bold & high contrast. Think black, white, icy blue, and jewel tones.
